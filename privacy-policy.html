1. Introduction

QuickWork Pro is a mobile application designed to help self-employed professionals create estimates and invoices. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in the app.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Financial data
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or usage data

All data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server or third party.

3. Offline Usage

QuickWork Pro is designed to work completely offline. No internet connection is required to use core features of the app.

4. Data You Enter

You may enter client names, job details, prices, or notes into the app. This information is stored only on your device. If you delete the app, this data will also be deleted unless you have manually backed it up.

5. Sharing Data

When you choose to share an invoice or estimate (e.g., via email or PDF), the content is shared only with your selected contact. We do not access or track this data in any way.

6. No Third-Party Services

QuickWork Pro does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

7. Children's Privacy

QuickWork Pro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. If we make changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at owenwangsp@gmail.com.
